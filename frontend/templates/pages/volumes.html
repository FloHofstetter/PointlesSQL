UC Volumes are file-storage namespaces inside a catalog. Create your first volume:

  1. Docker: docker exec pointlessql-soyuz \ soyuz-cli volumes create --catalog <name> --schema <name> --volume <name>
  2. Python: client.volumes.create(catalog_name=…, schema_name=…, name=…)
  3. Hermes: agent tool create_volume(catalog, schema, name)

Once a volume has a file:// storage location, PointlesSQL can accept uploads and convert files to Delta tables.
