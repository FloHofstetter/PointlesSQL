{# Revision history + cell-diff drawer. Lives outside the per-cell ``x-for`` so it stays single-instance. Toggled by the toolbar "History" button via ``toggleRevisionsPanel()``. Left pane: a list of revisions (newest first) with a "Pin" button per row. Right pane: the diff envelope between the two pinned revisions. Source-cell diffs are rendered as a naive line-by-line unified diff via ``renderUnifiedDiff(old, new)`` — good enough for the typical cell-source delta until a Monaco / CodeMirror-merge side-by-side editor lands behind the same panel. #}
