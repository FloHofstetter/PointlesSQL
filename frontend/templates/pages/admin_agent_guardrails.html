Agent guardrails

Contextual service policies decide whether an agent action is allowed, blocked, or must wait for human approval — based on who is acting and the model, MCP service, tool and content involved. Author the rules as Cedar modules on the Policy modules page, then evaluate a hypothetical action here to see how they fire. A guardrail that forbids Action::"agent_action" but permits Action::"agent_action_with_approval" routes the action through review rather than blocking it; a plain forbid blocks it. With no modules linked, every action is allowed.
